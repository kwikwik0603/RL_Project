	ability - 1. 3 sections of health, but after each section of health -> create a shield of 1/2 health 
	2. create a stone wall like Aether's Geo ult ( Genshin )

	2. Whirlwind, drags the character to the eye of the storm and attack them, slows the character down and makes it difficult to dodge.
